Ultimate Weekly Plan for Super Elite Swimming Performance

Weekly Overview

Your path to SUPER elite level:

  • Hit 5+ swim sessions (3 Worksop, 2 lane)
  • 3 strength sessions + 2 core-specific workouts
  • Daily stretching/mobility for recovery
  • Full food prep & high-quality whole foods
  • Study 2 subjects minimum daily
